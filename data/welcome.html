Thanks for installing the add-on

The Test4Theory add-on has been successfully installed

Now you have to configure it with your Test4Theory BOINC ID:

  • Open the Add-ons menu of Firefox (click in Tools → Add-ons),
  • Click in the Extensions tab
  • Press the Test4Theory extension preferences button for adding your ID,
  • Replace the T4T BOINC ID: empty string with your Test4Theory BOINC ID
  • Close the Add-ons tab, and you should start receiving notifications.

Tip You can also edit the frequency that you want to check for new notifications.
